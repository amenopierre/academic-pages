Welcome to my webpage!

I am a PhD student at POEMS in Palaiseau. My supervisors are Sonia Fliss and Patrick Joly.

Located at the interface between theoretical analysis and numerics, my PhD thesis deals with time-harmonic scalar wave propagation in heterogeneous media. The goal is to develop original numerical methods to solve the Helmholtz equation in unbounded structures involving quasiperiodicity.
